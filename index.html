Jianan Zhang

 


I am a senior software engineer at Google Cloud. I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Communications and Networks at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT), advised by Professor Eytan Modiano. I obtained my bachelor's degree from Tsinghua University in 2012 and master's degree from MIT in 2014.

I am broadly interested in network theory, with focus on robust network designs and resource allocations, with applications to interdependent networks, cyber physical systems, and data center networks.
