Jianan Zhang

 


I am a graduate student at Laboratory for Information and Decision Systems, Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT). My adviser is Professor Eytan Modiano. I obtained my bachelor's degree from Tsinghua University in 2012 and master's degree from MIT in 2014.

My research interests include modeling and designing reliable networks, developing techniques in optimization and graph theory. I am currently working on modeling and analysis of interdependent networks.
